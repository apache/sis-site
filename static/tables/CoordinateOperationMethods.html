Apache SIS™ Coordinate Operation Methods

The following tables summarize the coordinate operation methods known to Apache SIS 1.3 together with the recognized parameters. There is three kinds of parameters:

  • The semi-major and semi-minor parameters are needed for all map projections, but usually do not need to be specified explicitly since they are inferred from the ellipsoid (unless creating parameterized transforms directly). For this reason, those parameters are usually not shown in EPSG repository or Well Known Text (WKT) definitions.
  • The earth_radius and inverse_flattening parameters (not shown below) are implicitly supported by all map projections. They are other ways to specify the ellipsoid (actually rarely used). Read and write operations on those implicit parameters are converted into equivalent operations on semi-major and semi-minor parameters.
  • Unless otherwise noticed, all other parameters are mandatory (in the sense that they should always be shown in forms, regardless of whether they have default value).
